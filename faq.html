1. Where is InVibe Festival taking place?

InVibe Festival will be held at 3 Route de la Muette Ã  Neuilly, 75116 Paris, in the Bois de Boulogne. This beautiful location offers a perfect blend of nature and music, creating an unforgettable summer experience.

2. How do I get to the festival? Is there parking available?

We encourage festival-goers to use public transport, ride-shares, or our official shuttle service. Limited on-site parking will be available for an additional fee. You can find detailed transportation options on the "program" page.

3. Can I bring my own food and drinks?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed inside the festival. However, we offer a diverse selection of food vendors, including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Free water stations will be available, so bring a reusable bottle!

4. Is alcohol available for purchase?

Yes! We have licensed bars offering a variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages. You must be 18+ (or the legal drinking age in your country) and provide valid ID to purchase alcohol.

5. What items are prohibited at the festival?

For everyone's safety, prohibited items include outside food & drinks, large bags, professional cameras, drones, weapons, illegal substances, fireworks, and glass containers. A full list of restricted items will be available on our website.

6. Will there be lockers or storage areas for personal belongings?

Yes, we provide secure lockers on-site so you can store your belongings safely while enjoying the festival. Lockers can be reserved in advance or rented on the day, subject to availability.

7. What happens if it rains? Will the festival still take place?

InVibe Festival is a rain-or-shine event! In case of extreme weather, safety measures will be implemented, and any updates will be communicated via our official channels. Dress accordingly and be prepared for all conditions!

8. Can I pay by cash?

Cash is not accepted on-site. You can pay for your drinks and food by card or contactless with your cell phone.

9. Will there be charging stations for phones and devices?

Yes! We will have charging stations available so you can keep your devices powered throughout the festival. Some may require a small fee, so bring a power bank if possible!
