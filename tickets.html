Tickets & Refund Policy

All tickets grant access to the festival grounds, live performances, food & drink vendors, and other on-site activities.

Important Ticket Information

All ticket sales are final—please ensure you select the correct ticket type before purchasing.

Tickets are digital and must be presented via mobile device or printed copy at the entrance.

Your ticket is personal and non-transferable. ID verification may be required upon entry.

The festival is a cashless event, so be prepared to pay with card or contactless payment methods.

Refund & Ticket Change Policy

Refunds: Tickets are non-refundable, except in the case of event cancellation. If the festival is canceled due to unforeseen circumstances, ticket holders will be notified and offered a refund or a ticket transfer to a future edition.

Ticket Changes: Upgrades from a single-day pass to a two-day pass may be possible, subject to availability. Contact customer support for assistance. Name changes on tickets are not allowed to prevent resale fraud.

Weather Conditions: The festival is a rain-or-shine event. Refunds will not be issued due to weather-related inconveniences.
